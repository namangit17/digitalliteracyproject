**Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist**

### ✅ **Do’s**

* **Pause before you post something**
  A quick second thought can save you from posting something you might regret later.

* **Show what you’d be okay being known for**
  Think of your profile as your digital personality—keep it something you’re proud of.

* **Check if something is true before sharing**
  It takes a few seconds to verify, but it helps stop fake news from spreading.

* **Be respectful in comments and messages**
  Disagreeing is fine, but there’s no need to be rude or aggressive.

* **Keep an eye on your privacy settings**
  It’s worth checking who can actually see your posts and personal info.

---

### ❌ **Don’ts**

* **Don’t share everything about your life**
  Some things are better kept private, especially your location or personal details.

* **Don’t post when you’re angry or upset**
  Emotional posts often don’t age well and can create unnecessary problems.

* **Don’t get involved in online drama**
  It rarely ends well and usually wastes your time and energy.

* **Don’t depend on likes for confidence**
  Social media numbers don’t define your value.

* **Don’t assume deleted means gone forever**
  Once something is online, it can easily be saved or shared by others.
